District-level Math Olympiad (DMO) Guide

The DMO is the first stage of the Math Olympiad journey in Nepal. It is usually held around December, but dates vary for each district. Registration can be done at the venue for convenience. Venue and date will be announced prior to the DMO.

FAQ

Q: Can we use a calculator?
A: Of course, calculators are allowed.

Format

  • 50 multiple-choice questions (MCQs)
  • Time: 100 minutes
  • Cutoff: 25 marks to qualify for PMO
  • No negative marking; attempt all questions

Tips

  • Review basic math formulas before DMO.
  • Stay calm; don’t panic during the exam.
  • Manage time wisely. Don’t rush answers but also don’t leave questions unanswered.
