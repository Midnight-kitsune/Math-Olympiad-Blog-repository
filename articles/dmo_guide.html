DMO Overview

NPLIMO-Volunteer: Prasiddha Subedi

The District Mathematical Olympiad (DMO) serves as the first step for students across Nepal aspiring to participate in the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO). This guide provides an overview of DMO structure, question pattern, and preparation resources.

DMO Structure

  • First round of Olympiad selection in Nepal
  • Organized by the Nepal Mathematical Society (NMS)
  • Includes both subjective and objective questions

Preparation Tips

  • Study past questions carefully and understand key concepts.
  • Practice logical reasoning and number theory.
  • Focus on proofs, geometry, and combinatorics.
