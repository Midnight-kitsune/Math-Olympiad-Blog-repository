DMO Guide

NPLIMO-Volunteer: Prasiddha Subedi

First Stage: DMO (District-level Math Olympiad)

  • Date: Around December (varies by district)
  • Venue: Announced before DMO
  • Registration: On-site registration possible
  • Calculator: Allowed
  • Questions: 50 MCQs
  • Time: 100 minutes
  • Cutoff: 25 marks to qualify for PMO

Tips:

  • Review basic math formulas before the exam
  • Do not panic; stay calm during DMO
  • Take care of time; do not rush answers but check carefully
  • Do not leave questions; no negative marking system
