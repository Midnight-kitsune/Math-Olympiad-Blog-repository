Date and Venue

  • DMO usually takes place around December, but the exact date may vary for different districts.
  • Venue and date will be announced before the DMO.
  • You can also register at the venue for convenience.

FAQ

Q: Can we use a calculator?

A: Of course! Calculators are allowed.

Exam Details

  • Number of Questions: 50 MCQs
  • Time: 100 minutes
  • Cutoff: 25 marks
  • Selection: Students who pass the cutoff will be selected for the Province-level Math Olympiad (PMO).

Tips for DMO

  1. Review basic math formulas before going to DMO.
  2. Stay calm—do not panic or worry during the exam.
  3. Manage your time carefully. Time may be an issue, but do not rush and mark answers too quickly. Sometimes options can be confusing, so check carefully.
  4. Answer all questions—there is no negative marking system, so leaving questions unanswered is not a good idea.

Good luck! Stay confident and enjoy solving problems. DMO is your first step toward the higher-level Math Olympiads.
