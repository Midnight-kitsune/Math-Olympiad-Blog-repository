Help for IMO (International Mathematics Olympiad)

Introduction

The IMO is the premier international mathematics competition for high school students. Questions are challenging yet beginner-friendly if you prepare step by step. Students participate representing their country and gain valuable learning experiences.

Benefits of Participating in IMO

  • Enhances your resume and academic profile.
  • Provides international exposure and recognition.
  • Builds creativity and mental growth.
  • Develops problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Opens opportunities for learning, collaboration, and scholarships.

Participating in IMO from Nepal

There are 4 stages:

  • DMO: Covered in DMO Guide
  • PMO: Covered in PMO Guide
  • P-TST: Subjective proof-based questions based on IMO level problems.
  • TST: Final selection test for the IMO team.

FAQ

  • Who can give IMO? Under 20 years old and not yet in university.
  • When can I start preparation? You can start DMO preparation from class 7 or 8. Improve every year for higher levels. Class 8 students can even reach NMO with practice.
  • I'm in class 10/11/12. Am I late? No, you can start as soon as you learn about it.
