Help for PMO (Province Level Mathematics Olympiad)

The PMO is the second stage of the Math Olympiad journey in Nepal. It is held after DMO is completed in every district of Nepal. It is held on the same date all around Nepal. Different venues and dates are announced before PMO. Registration can be done at the venue for convenience.

FAQ

Q: Can we use a calculator?
A: Yes, calculators are allowed.

Q: What to bring with us?
A: Geometry box (including all items might be helpful) and calculator.

Format (Last Year)

  • 35 Multiple Choice Questions (1 mark each)
  • 5 Numerical Questions (5 marks each) – give numerical answer, no MCQs
  • Cutoff: Marks obtained by 100th ranked competitor from all around Nepal. They will be eligible for P-TST (Pre-Team Selection Test)
  • No negative marking
  • Time: 100 minutes

Tips

  • Reach the venue on time.
  • Stay calm, relax, and stay hydrated.
  • Manage your time wisely and don’t panic.
